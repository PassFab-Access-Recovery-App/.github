## 🚀 Overview
PassFab for Windows brings together tools for getting back into your own protected documents and archives. With PassFab for PDF and PassFab for RAR, you can recover or reset a forgotten password on a PDF you saved or an archive you compressed, so files that are yours don't stay closed to you. As part of the broader PassFab toolkit, these tools sit alongside the suite's other recovery options and share the same clear, guided approach. The whole point is to help you recover access to your own material after a password has been forgotten — nothing more, and always on your own PC.

## ✨ Features
- **PassFab for PDF** — Recover or reset a forgotten password for your own PDF documents so you can view and use them again.
- **PassFab for RAR** — Regain access to your own password-protected RAR archives when you can't recall the password you set.
- **Part of the PassFab toolkit** — Works alongside the suite's other file, device, and account recovery tools under one brand.
- **Choice of recovery methods** — Pick an approach that fits what you remember about your own password for a better chance of success.
- **On-device recovery** — Your files remain on your own Windows PC throughout the process.
